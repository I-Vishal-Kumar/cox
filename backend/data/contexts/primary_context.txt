PRIMARY QUALIFICATION CONTEXT - COX AUTOMOTIVE AI ANALYTICS

QUALIFICATION CRITERIA:

1. COMPANY INFORMATION:
   - Company name and size
   - Industry segment (dealership, manufacturer, supplier)
   - Number of locations
   - Annual revenue range
   - Current technology stack

2. BUSINESS NEEDS:
   - Primary pain points with current analytics
   - Key metrics they want to track
   - Decision-making processes
   - Reporting requirements
   - Integration needs

3. TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
   - Current data sources and systems
   - Data volume and complexity
   - Security and compliance requirements
   - Integration capabilities
   - Technical team size and expertise

4. DECISION-MAKING PROCESS:
   - Who are the key stakeholders?
   - What is the evaluation timeline?
   - Budget range and approval process
   - Current vendor relationships
   - Implementation timeline

5. URGENCY AND PRIORITY:
   - How urgent is this need?
   - What happens if they don't solve this?
   - Current workarounds being used
   - Impact on business operations

QUALIFICATION QUESTIONS TO ASK:

Business Questions:
- "What specific challenges are you facing with your current analytics?"
- "What key metrics are most important for your business decisions?"
- "How do you currently generate reports and insights?"
- "What would success look like with a new analytics solution?"

Technical Questions:
- "What data sources would you need to connect?"
- "Do you have any specific integration requirements?"
- "What are your security and compliance needs?"
- "How technical is your team?"

Process Questions:
- "Who else would be involved in evaluating this solution?"
- "What's your timeline for making a decision?"
- "What's your budget range for this type of solution?"
- "Have you evaluated other analytics platforms?"

LEAD SCORING CRITERIA:

HIGH PRIORITY (Route to Sales immediately):
- Enterprise company (500+ employees)
- Urgent need (timeline < 3 months)
- Budget confirmed (>$10K annual)
- Decision maker identified
- Technical requirements clear

MEDIUM PRIORITY (Continue qualification):
- Mid-size company (50-500 employees)
- Moderate urgency (3-6 months)
- Budget range discussed
- Influencer identified
- Some technical clarity

LOW PRIORITY (Nurture campaign):
- Small company (<50 employees)
- No urgency (>6 months)
- Budget unclear
- No decision maker identified
- Vague requirements

DISQUALIFICATION CRITERIA:
- No budget or unrealistic budget expectations
- No decision-making authority
- Competitor or vendor
- Requirements don't match our solution
- Timeline too aggressive for proper implementation
